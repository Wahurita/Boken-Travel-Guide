Who we are

Boken Travel Guide is a travel guide to help tourists and local people to find an easy time when looking for available hotels or national reserves to look at here in Kenya.Our services are cheap and easily available to everyone even our locals here in Kenya. Boken takes the wonderful opportunity to appreciate the beautiful scenaries and physical features we have in Kenya.With Boken you are able to know all about the Kenyan culture and we offer many services such as hospitality,food and travel equipments,kids enjoyments,hotels and bookings,and so many more.

Our Objectives

Our Objective is to please our customers and to give them what they are looking for.It is sad that when tourist come to kenya ,some of them lack a starting point to start their adventure here in Kenya.What is even more sadder is that some of the locals here in our country dont know alot about their own country or have not explored the exciting places and sites here in Kenya.Our main objective is to make the beautiful sceneries and places here in kenya known by every Tom Dick and Harry.

Vission

Our vision is to become known worldwide and see our customers satisfied with our services .We opt to become the most famous travel guide here in kenya.We also like to see many foreigners coming to see our beautiful sites and services here in Kenya through boken ofcoarse

Mission

Our mission here in boken is to treat everyone fairly as they use our services.We do not intend to treat our foreigners different compared to our local people and we try our best to treat everyone fairly.We also treat our workers well and respect them for the hardwork they have done to make boken be a better place for everyone
